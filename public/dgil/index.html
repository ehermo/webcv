About me

I've always known I wanted to work on something related to technology and I've always been interested in the world of communications: these are the main things which have motivated me to study Telecommunication Engineering.

My first work experience in Vodafone helped me gaining insight of the world of Tier 1 Operators and encouraged me to continue working in the sector. Thereafter my work in Optare Solutions allowed me to play different roles in OSS projects: Programmer, Analyst and, from early 2012, OSM Consultant.

I have and international profile and have developed the skills to work in onsite and remote cross-cultural teams. I possess good communication skills, with both the client and the team and among my professional and personal traits stand out: leadership and organization skills, learning abilities, capacity for coping with pressure and level of commitment.
